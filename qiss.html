QIS College of Engineering

QIS College of Engineering & Technology, an Autonomous leader established in 1998, stands as a beacon of quality education in Ongole. Accredited with NAAC A+, we provide an ecosystem where innovation meets discipline, ensuring our students are equipped with world-class technical skills and professional ethics to lead the global tech industry.
